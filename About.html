About

Our Mission

We strive to provide the most up to date information about Physical Science, all for the love of learning something new. We proudly give curious people the freedom to learn about Physical Science here.

Our Values

At our core, BEC Media Group©, operates on hardwork, commitment, and perseverance. We keep the presence of these values the whole duration of making this website, and this is evident in the way that this whole website is made.

Our Philosophy

Our main principle in making this website is to ensure that every information is up to date, credible, and relevant in today's standards. And no, we didn't just plagiarize it from other websites... We wouldn't do that.

Our Vision

We are looking forward to expand this venture, and reach towards possibly making a website about everything that we learned in Science thus far, but since that is too much work, I think I will lay off that one for a while.
